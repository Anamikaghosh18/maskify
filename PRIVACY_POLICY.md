# Privacy Policy for Maskify

Last Updated: May 10, 2026

## Introduction
Maskify ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome Extension.

## Data Collection
1. **User Preferences**: We store your blurring settings (blur intensity, active status) on our backend servers to allow for cross-device synchronization.
2. **No Message Content Stored**: Maskify **never** reads, stores, or transmits the content of your messages. All blurring happens locally on your machine within your browser.
3. **No Personal Information**: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information (PII).

## Data Usage
Your preferences are used solely to provide and maintain the synchronization feature of the extension.

## Data Security
We implement standard security measures to protect your preference data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## Third-Party Services
Maskify does not share your data with any third-party services.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
